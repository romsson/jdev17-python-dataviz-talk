Visualization as.. a combinatorial problemÂ¶

Line, Circle, Rectangle, .. (Graphical Marks)

$\times$ Position, Size, Color, .. (Properties)

$\times$ Selection, Binning, Aggregation, .. (Data transformation)

$=$ Huge number of permutations (even for small and simple datasets)

What to optimize? How to evaluate?Â¶

  • Some principles allow to reduce the size of the space and directly jump to interesting points

  • Otherwise, no automatic rules to generate a visualization: each visualization has a specific role, efficiency in a given context.
